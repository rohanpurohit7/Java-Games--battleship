# Game Narrative

## Premise

The player commands a torpedo control station during a compact naval engagement. Three submarine fleet groups are hidden beneath a 7x7 battle grid. The mission is to locate and sink every fleet before the torpedo supply runs out.

## Fleet Commanders

The game uses three fleet identities with distinct visual colors and spoken reactions:

- Admiral Isoroku Yamamoto: Japanese Fleet
- Admiral Horatio Nelson: English Fleet
- Admiral Karl Doenitz: German Fleet

These are used as game characters and fleet labels. The visual design uses broad naval-inspired palettes only and avoids political insignia.

## Voice Behavior

Each fleet has a different spoken response style:

- Japanese Fleet: measured and severe
- English Fleet: formal and defiant
- German Fleet: colder and lower in tone

Voice lines trigger when:

- A fleet is hit
- A fleet is sunk
- The player wins
- The player runs out of torpedoes

## Mission Levels

Difficulty changes the pressure on the player by limiting torpedoes:

- Easy: 15 torpedoes
- Medium: 13 torpedoes
- High: 10 torpedoes

The fleets remain hidden at every level.
